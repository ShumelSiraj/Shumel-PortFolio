About Me

I am Shumel Siraj, a Data Science professional whose career trajectory is a testament to the synergistic power of mathematics, business acumen, and advanced analytics. My journey began with a Bachelor of Science Honors in Mathematics from Aligarh Muslim University, where I developed a strong foundation in analytical thinking and problem-solving. This quantitative background was the bedrock upon which I built my business understanding through a Master of Business Administration in International Business from Jamia Millia Islamia University. The MBA experience broadened my perspective, enabling me to appreciate the intricate dance between data-driven decisions and business strategy.

Transitioning to a Master of Science in Data Science at The George Washington University was a natural progression, merging my love for numbers with the dynamic field of data science. Here, I honed my skills in predictive modeling, machine learning, and data management, preparing me for impactful roles such as a Data Science Consultant at The World Bank Group and a Material Management Analyst at The George Washington University. This educational and professional journey has not only enhanced my analytical capabilities but also empowered me with a holistic view of how data influences and drives business outcomes. Today, I stand as a professional who embodies the integration of mathematical precision, strategic business insight, and cutting-edge data science, committed to leveraging this unique blend to drive organizational success and innovation.
